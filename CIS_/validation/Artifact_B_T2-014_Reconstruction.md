# Artifact B — T2-014 ValuJet Flight 592

**Status:** FROZEN | **Date:** 2026-06-02 | **Artifact A:** SEALED  
**Sources:** NTSB AAR-97/06 (1997)

---

## Construct Determinations

**CDA — PRESENT | HIGH:** The generators' actual operational state (active, not discharged) was known to SabreTech workers but did not cross into ValuJet's cargo acceptance process. The "empty" labelling substituted false information for correct information. The critical state information was absent from the cargo decision context at every relevant checkpoint. Confirmed HIGH.

**LD — PRESENT | HIGH:** SabreTech's responsibility for safe generator preparation was transferred to ValuJet's cargo process without adequate handoff of information or capacity for ValuJet to independently verify generator status. ValuJet's cargo acceptance relied entirely on SabreTech's labelling. The risk was displaced to a context without evaluation capacity. Confirmed HIGH.

**CR — PRESENT | MODERATE:** The NTSB investigation found evidence that SabreTech workers knew the generators were not properly prepared. The decision to label them "empty" and ship them represented a closure — the concern was resolved by relabelling rather than by actual discharge and capping. Options Released: proper preparation. Confirmed MODERATE.

**HCL — PRESENT | MODERATE:** SabreTech workers, ValuJet cargo acceptance, and FAA oversight were three organisationally independent domains each holding incomplete information about the generator situation. The shared structural source — the generators' actual operational state — was simultaneously relevant to all three domains without any domain synthesising the cross-domain picture. Confirmed MODERATE.

**SI-1 — ABSENT | HIGH:** Confirmed. The cargo specification was correct — it excluded active hazardous materials. The specification assumption was right. The failure was incorrect information admitted to the specification-governed process (CDA), not a specification assumption about adjacent system behaviour being wrong. The falsification condition (specification assumed verification capability that didn't exist) is not met — the specification didn't assume ValuJet could independently verify generator status; it assumed materials would be correctly represented. That the representation was false is CDA and LD, not SI-1. Confirmed absent.

**WSP — ABSENT | HIGH:** Specific instance, not accumulated pattern. Confirmed.  
**FA — PRESENT | MODERATE:** Multiple degraded organisational conditions confirmed.
